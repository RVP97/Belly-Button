Bacteria in Bellybutton: Nasty or helpful?
Data Analyst creates an interactive dashboard


How is the washing frequency of you belly button correlated to the kind and amount of bacteria found in it? Have you ever wondered about this? Well, we have. And it is why we have created an interactive dashboard that facilitates the visualization of different correlations.

Three charts are displayed on this dashboard, providing information on test subjects regarding different metrics. The first bar graph provides details about the most prominent bacteria cultures found on the subject's belly button. There is a gauge wash, which is pretty self explanatory. It includes an indicator which tells how it compares with the average washing frequency of the sample group. Lastly, there is a bubble plot, graphically displaying the information in a neat manner.
